# Emozzk Lite Privacy Policy

Emozzk Lite does not collect, sell, transmit, or share personal information.

The extension stores only functional settings in the user's browser local storage, such as shortcut settings, favorite emote identifiers, recent emote settings, and UI preferences.

This data remains on the user's device and is not sent to any external server.

Emozzk Lite does not collect chat messages, login information, cookies, browsing history, payment information, health information, location information, or personally identifiable information.

Emozzk Lite is not an official CHZZK or NAVER extension.
